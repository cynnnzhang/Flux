ABOUT ME

My name’s Cynthia, but for short I go by Cyn. I’m a total enthusiast for design, architecture, and travelling to new places. To me, a perfect trip is defined by sore feet, and a newfound understanding of a cities society and culture. In my lifetime I’ve been lucky enough to walk along peaceful canals, hike through lush forests, stand in bustling town squares, climb up towering edifices, and dip my feet into glistening ocean water. I’ve cherished every moment where I’ve been able to escape small town Waterloo where I grew up. I find new peace, liberty, and inspiration while enveloped in new spaces and environments, and develop new feelings incomparable from one city to the next. My aim for this website is to share these feelings with you, and help you make your trips fulfilling and unforgettable. I want to be that adventurous friend you catch up with over a cup of coffee, and that friend who allows you to see the world through a different lense.
